## BIO:

Duke, otherwise known as Obtuse Angler by Midi, is a very wide and heavy hidegvin working for the Cold Front. He works as the chef aboard the Unyielding Winter. Generally one of the more well-mannered members of the faction, Duke is very simple-minded, often thinking about food and not much else. He enjoys fishing, and uses a high-test line that he can also use to grapple and swing in combat, using himself like a wrecking ball.

## GAMEPLAY:

As a boss, Duke will swing from grapple points and then roll across the battlefield. He'll also attempt to jump and crush enemies under him, or grapple them towards him to roll on top of them. A large enough object can be caught in his line to have him snag it instead and hit himself.
