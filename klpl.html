Preamble


This Preamble explains the philosophy and objectives underlying the Kyrodian Legends Public License (KLPL). It is provided for interpretive guidance only and does not create additional rights or obligations beyond those set forth in the operative terms of this License.

Throughout most of human history, stories functioned as shared cultural inheritance. Myths, legends, and characters evolved as they were retold, expanded, reinterpreted, and continued by successive generations. No single author possessed exclusive authority over the tale of a hero, the fate of a kingdom, or the future of a world. Narrative traditions were living systems.

Modern copyright law altered that structure by enabling exclusive control over fictional worlds and characters. While such control can incentivize creation, it can also result in cultural enclosure: works that might otherwise evolve through collective participation become fixed, centralized, and guarded. In some cases, adaptations of public domain works have become so culturally dominant that earlier forms are obscured, and newly added elements are mistaken for shared heritage.

The KLPL is designed to respond to this dynamic. It seeks to preserve Kyrodian Legends Content (KLC) as a living body of work that may be freely used, expanded, and continued by others. It permits commercial use, adaptation, and redistribution. It does not seek to prevent profit. Rather, it seeks to prevent a situation in which profit depends on withholding KLC itself from downstream reuse.

This License applies copyleft obligations to KLC and to modifications or derivative works of KLC, ensuring that additions to the shared world remain available to future participants. At the same time, it is not intended to impose licensing requirements on independent works that merely incorporate KLC as a separable component, provided the conditions of separability described in this License are satisfied.

The objective of the KLPL is not to eliminate authorship, but to cultivate continuity. It is intended to allow any person to take up the narrative torch, extend the world in new directions, and contribute to an evolving mythology that remains open rather than enclosed.
