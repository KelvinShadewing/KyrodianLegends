# Technology

Kyroda's technology is limited to early 20th century levels. This stagnation doesn't extend to cultural and stylistic evolution, but things like TV, internet, and high-density digital displays don't exist. Most electronics are radio-based.

## Airships

Airships are powered by hydrogen fuel. Fossil fuels were used, but economic pressure from [Kholua](kholu.md) for green energy along with [Marstelos'](marstels.md) valuing science over profit ended up pushing older fuel sources out of most of the market, especially with Marstelos innovating transparent aluminum windows and titanium structures to produce lighter and more durable ships.

Older models rely on balloons, but since the mining of skylands brought levitite into the ecosystem, airships have begun using levite crystals for bouyancy. This has reduced drag on the ships and allowed tremendous increases in maximum speed.

## Power Crystals

One [biometal](biometals.md) component that tends to survive their detonation is a diamond core composed of an artificial carbon lattice with mild radioactive elements. The radiation is too low to harm organic matter. These diamonds can be charged with electricity to be used as batteries.

Quartz-based magic variants exist that store mana which can be drawn to power enchanted artifacts. Enchantments, however, cannot be industrially produced due to magic requiring intent to function, meaning they must be hand-crafted.
