# Bosses

## ==== Biometals ====

### Cortex Vespa

* **Type:** Semi-flying
* **Attacks:** Stinger needle (2 damage, rapid shot, grabbable), summon pillbug, summon hoverbee
* **Health:** 500

Large queen bee biometal. Her abdomen is a data processing server. She can control other biometals remotely and will summon them to defend her. Her needle spikes can be grabbed and thrown back.

She is technically a traitor to other biometals. She created the transphere and programmed it to bind with Kyrodians who come near, looking for one who will make a compatible match.

### Aspis Adder

* **Type:** Defensive
* **Attacks:** Shielded roll, lunging strike, acid shot
* **Health:** 200

A heavily armored snake that rolls across the arena and attempts to bite or spit at the player. Its head is vulnarable when not rolling.

### Rapid Wilddog (Trigger Happyena)

* **Type:** Gunner
* **Attacks:** Machine gun, aerial shot, Z jump, heavy rocket
* **Health:** 400

An anthro hyena. Heavily armed with wrist and shoulder-mounted guns. Recurring enemy who befriends [Vorbis](vorbis.md). He has a very gun-happy personality and actually likes the nickname Midi gives him, much to Midi's displeasure.

In battle, he'll jump between the walls of the arena, firing bullets from above as he goes.

### Hurricane Seagull (Stormy Gull)

* **Type:** Flying
* **Attacks:** Tornado hold, tornado shot, divebomber command
* **Health:** 200
* **Attributes:** Air element

Encountered on a skyland. He can control bird biometals to aid him in battle. Hunter can grab him, but he'll use a tornado and send both careening. If you let go at the last moment, he can be made to crash when trying to slam Hunter. Earth attacks will jam his turbines and ground him, also preventing his tornado thrashing when held by Hunter for a short time.

### Artisan Nezumi (Paint Brux)

* **Type:** Speed
* **Attacks:** Physics paint, electric current
* **Health:** 200
* **Attributes:** Shock element

Paints the environment with gel that affects its properties, such as making it slippery, sticky, or bouncy. Globs of gel act as projectile attacks when airborne. Will flee at half health, after which Midi will learn the water bomb to counter Nezumi's attacks. When desperate, he will switch to electrified gel that carries a current through any surface he's touching.

## ==== Cold Front ====

### [Emperor Pendragon](pendragon.md)

* **Type:** Heavy
* **Attacks:** Spinning slash, rolling slash, ice sword beam, blizzard pillar
* **Health:** 1000, fire damage x 2.0, ice damage x 0.0
* **Attributes:** Unfreezeable

Will parry some ranged attacks with his sword. When low on health, blizzard pillar becomes a desperation move, stretching out wide. It can be countered with a charged fire bomb.

If [Hunter](hunter.md) shinesparks into him, he will attempt to block it, forcing Hunter to mash attack to break his guard. Successfully breaking it will do major damage.

* **Health:** 300

## ==== Vampire Candidates/Lupin's Monsters ====

### Giant Plant

* **Type:** Stationairy
* **Attacks:** Seed shot, vine whip, root stab, chomp
* **Health:** 200

A giant plant that is pre-seeded by Marie Annette and her thrall as a first phase. It will fire seeds and attempt to devour the player. While one character is in its mouth, the other will be playable and can attack until it releases them.

### Flint Mistmane

* **Type:** Flying
* **Attacks:** Draining bite, lunge, fog
* **Health:** 400

Uses fog to obfuscate the arena and attempts to sneak up on the player to bite them and steal health. At low health, he uses his stolen bat form to evade and rush at the player for more health.
