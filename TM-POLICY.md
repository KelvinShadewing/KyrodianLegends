## 0. Preamble and Summary

Kyrodian Legends is designed as an open universe. This trademark policy exists to preserve clarity for audiences and protect the shared identity of the setting, not to restrict creative participation.

Good faith is evaluated based on objective compliance with this Policy and not solely on stated intent.

The rules below are written in structured legal language because trademark law requires clarity and consistency in order to preserve validity. They are not intended to impose editorial control or creative hierarchy.

If you are creating in good faith and communicating transparently, this policy is unlikely to present obstacles.

**What You Can Do**

You may:

* Create and distribute works that incorporate KLC under the KLPL.

* Use the name “Kyrodian Legends” in your title.

* Optionally use the official logo (without modifying it).

**What You Must Do**

1. Clearly identify yourself as the creator.

2. Include a disclaimer such as:

> “This is an independent work set in the Kyrodian Legends universe and is not an official release or endorsed by the original creators.”

3. Do not imply official endorsement, sponsorship, or canon authority.

4. Do not modify the official logo except for resizing.

5. Do not present the brand in a way that would confuse a reasonable audience about who created or officially controls the work.

**What This Policy Is Not**

* It is not a canon enforcement system.

* It does not give the Owner editorial control over compliant works.

* It does not prohibit good-faith crossovers or collaborations.

* It is not intended to restrict creative experimentation.

The purpose of this policy is clarity so audiences understand who made what.

For the formal legal terms, see the following sections below.

## 1. Purpose of the Marks

The Marks identify the Kyrodian Legends universe and distinguish works that incorporate KLC from unrelated works. The Marks must not be used in a manner that is likely to cause consumer confusion about:

* Source

* Sponsorship

* Official canon status

* Endorsement

Use must not dilute the distinctiveness of the Marks under applicable trademark law.

----

## 2. Permitted Use of the Word Mark

You may use the word mark “Kyrodian Legends” in connection with a work that:

* Complies with the KLPL,

* Clearly incorporates KLC,

* Does not imply official endorsement.

2.1 Title Format

You may use the Mark in a title using the following general format:

> Kyrodian Legends: Subtitle
> or
> Subtitle – A Kyrodian Legends Story

The subtitle should meaningfully distinguish the work from others.

Use of a subtitle is strongly recommended and may be required if necessary to avoid confusion with existing works.

Titles must not be structured or stylized in a manner that causes the subtitle to be perceived as subordinate branding rather than a distinguishing identifier.

2.2 Creator Identification

The actual creator or studio name must appear clearly and with prominence reasonably comparable to the use of the Kyrodian Legends mark, such that a reasonable consumer can readily identify the independent creator.

Example:

> Kyrodian Legends: Samples of Compliance
> by Jane Doe

The goal is clarity of authorship, not uniform branding hierarchy.

----

## 3. Required Disclaimer

Where practical (books, websites, digital releases, packaging, storefront listings), you must include a disclaimer substantially similar to:

> “This is an independent work set in the Kyrodian Legends universe and is not an official release or endorsed by the original creators.”

The disclaimer must be reasonably legible and placed where a reasonable consumer would expect to find source-identifying information.

## 4. Logo Use

The following clarifies branding rules primarily relevant to large-scale commercial releases and cross-brand collaborations.

Use of the official Kyrodian Legends logo (“Logo”) is permitted only in connection with works that comply with the KLPL and this Policy.

The Logo may not be modified except for proportional scaling. It may not be recolored, distorted, cropped, retyped, redrawn, animated, separated into components, or otherwise altered.

The Logo must not be used in any manner that would reasonably be likely to cause consumer confusion regarding source, sponsorship, endorsement, partnership, or official canon status.

The Logo may be displayed alongside other trademarks or titles, including in crossover works, provided that:

* The Logo remains complete, unmodified, and legible;

* The Logo is recognizable to a reasonable consumer as a distinct and separate source identifier;

* The Logo is not combined with another mark in a manner that creates a single unified source identifier;

* The overall presentation does not materially increase the likelihood of consumer confusion regarding sponsorship or endorsement; and

* Any third-party trademarks are used in compliance with applicable law.

For purposes of this Policy, a “composite logo” means a presentation in which the Logo and another mark are integrated such that they function together as a single source identifier in the perception of a reasonable consumer.

Visual adjacency, contact, or partial overlap is permitted provided that:

* Each mark remains complete and unaltered,

* Each mark remains independently legible,

* The presentation does not create a likelihood of consumer confusion as to unified brand ownership.

Permission to use the Logo terminates upon material noncompliance with the KLPL or this Policy. “Material noncompliance” means a violation that would reasonably be expected to cause consumer confusion as to source, sponsorship, endorsement, or official status.

Termination affects permission to use the Marks, including the Logo and word mark, and does not alter copyright rights granted under the KLPL.

## 5. Prohibited Uses

You may not use the Marks:

* In connection with works that do not incorporate KLC.

* In a manner that suggests official canon authority unless explicitly designated.

* As the sole primary brand of a studio, publisher, or unrelated product line.

* On merchandise unrelated to a compliant KL work.

* In connection with unlawful, defamatory, or fraudulent content.

* In a manner that is misleading or likely to cause confusion.

* In a manner that presents a work as the primary, definitive, canonical, or authoritative continuation of Kyrodian Legends as a whole, regardless of whether explicit claims of endorsement are made.

* In a manner that, in the context of the broader ecosystem, would reasonably lead a typical audience to understand it as the central, default, or primary reference point for Kyrodian Legends as a whole.

* As the name or primary identifier of a centralized platform, index, or service that aggregates or represents multiple works in a manner that suggests unified authorship or canonical authority over the Kyrodian Legends setting.

* In the name of an organization, group, or initiative in a manner that suggests governance, official oversight, or authoritative stewardship of Kyrodian Legends as a whole.

Where works are selected, ranked, or curated, such curation must be presented as subjective or project-specific and must not be represented as defining the official, canonical, or preferred form of Kyrodian Legends.

This section is intended solely to preserve trademark validity and source-identifying function. It does not grant the Owner editorial control over the creative content of compliant works.
